The Vision

The main objective of Mobile and Social Computing Lab is to build predictive and computational approaches to human behavior understanding using the newly ubiquitous sources of data that are becoming available about all aspects of human life (e.g. mobile phone data, credit card transactions, energy consumption data, social media data from Facebook, Twitter, Foursquare, etc.). Moreover, MobS aims also at using these computational models of human behaviors in order to design more efficient companies, cities and societies, and to solve challenges in several fields such as health, finance, crime, transportation, energy consumption and sustainability, unemployment, etc.
